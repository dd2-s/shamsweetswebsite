Our Story

A Legacy of Sweet Excellence Since 1951

Sham's Legacy

Established in 1951 by Late Shri Sham Lal Bajaj, Sham Sweets has been a cornerstone of authentic Indian sweets and snacks in Gurgaon. The legacy was carried forward by Mr. Darshan Kumar Bajaj and is currently managed by Mr. Saheb Bajaj, maintaining the same commitment to quality and tradition.

What Makes Us Special

Sham Sweets stands out for its commitment to quality and tradition in Indian sweets. Here are our unique selling points:

  • Authentic Recipes – We use traditional methods to prepare sweets, ensuring rich flavors and textures.
  • Pure Ghee Sweets – Our sweets are made with pure desi ghee, enhancing taste and quality.
  • Wide Variety – From Dodha Burfi to Bengali sweets, we offer a diverse range of mithai.
  • Festive Specialties – We provide beautifully curated gift packs for celebrations.
  • Legacy & Trust – Established in 1951, we have built a reputation for excellence over decades.

Our Offerings

We specialize in a diverse range of traditional Indian sweets, snacks, and namkeen. Our menu includes:

  • Traditional Indian Sweets & Snacks
  • Ready Snacks & Quick Bites
  • Chatori Chaat
  • Multi-cuisine options: North Indian, Chinese, South Indian, and Continental
